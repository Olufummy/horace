HORACE Beverages:

Essence of Life in Every Sip!!!.

  • We are Introducing a new spirit drink such as Gin, Whisky, Vodka and Rum into the British Spirit and Beverages business landscape. Our dream and aspirations if for these to take over the world in the nearest future as we take a delve into other traditions and cultures to break new barriers with health-focused spirit drinks and some great natural plant juices that will cater for the increasing consumer demand for healthier beverage options
  • The beverage industry has become highly competitive, with many spirit drinks and fruit juices that saturate the market. This gives an opportunity for a unique spirit drink and plant-based fruit juice that will provide a healthier alternative, filled with innovative flavors, and traditional organics from the tropics of Africa, that would give amazing unique blends to new spirit drinks and plant juice. As the world and Consumers are becoming more aware of the environmental impact of their innovations and purchases.

Our Products

Horace Drinks are made from sustainably sourced ingredients, eco-friendly packaging, that will appeal to every lover of Spirit drinks, plant and fruit juice and environmentally conscious consumers. Our ethos is to remain a Small Holder company while we create and expand sustainable wealth. As well as working with local small holder farmers and local economies that often struggle due to market volatility and lack of demand. Our primary ingredients will be sourced locally and to support local agriculture, and create a positive brand story around community support and sustainability.
